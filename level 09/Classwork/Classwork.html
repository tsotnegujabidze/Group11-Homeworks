Soccer

Soccer, also known as football, is the world's most popular sport, played by over 250 million people in more than 200 countries. The game is played on a rectangular field with a goal at each end. Teams compete to score by getting a ball into the opposing goal using any part of their body except their hands and arms. The World Cup, held every four years, is the sport's most prestigious tournament, attracting billions of viewers. Soccer fosters teamwork, strategy, and physical fitness, making it a beloved pastime for many.
